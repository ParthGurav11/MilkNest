Our products

Fresh dairy made on site and sold daily. Descriptions are samples — update with your varieties and pricing.

Fresh milk

Whole and low-fat milk, pasteurized on site. Farm-bottled for everyday use.

Butter

Salted and unsalted butter, churned in small batches for a rich, creamy taste.

Cheese

Curds, blocks, and slices — mild to sharp. Ask about seasonal specials.

Paneer

Soft cottage-style cheese, ideal for curries, grilling, and snacks. Fresh blocks cut to order.

Buttermilk

Traditional cultured buttermilk — tangy, refreshing, and perfect for cooking or drinking chilled.

Lassi

Sweet or salted yogurt-based drink, whisked smooth. A cooling favorite with meals or on its own.

Khava / Mava

Reduced milk solids (khoya/mawa) for sweets like barfi, peda, and gulab jamun — fresh and aromatic.

Ghee

Clarified butter with a nutty aroma — ideal for cooking, tadka, and traditional recipes.
